Desire

"Fight For Me"

Although she is being forced to leave it's clear that the author wants nothing more than to be wanted. Constantly being uprooted allows for her to grow distraught and develop a contradicting persona of wanting to belong but knowing her place is not here with everyone else. This causes emotional strain. Her desires can not be met fully because it is out of her own hands.
